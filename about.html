✨ About Me ✨

Khamagani! From the vibrant Pink City of Jaipur — where heritage and colours breathe life into everyday stories — I'm Pallavi Singh: a banker turned hotelier, and today, a coach by calling.

My journey with coaching didn't begin in a seminar hall — it began with my curiosity about people and their stories. The why behind habits, relationships, and human behaviour fascinated me. Across two decades in diverse careers, one thread stayed constant — helping people grow.

But my real transformation began when life tested me deeply.

In my late twenties and thirties, I faced a storm of relationship complexities, family pressures, deceit, and emotional exhaustion. Anxiety gnawed at me; sleepless nights became routine. There were moments I believed it would never end.

Yet life had a different plan. It was pushing me not to break — but to awaken.

Step by patient step, I rebuilt myself: through meditation, fitness, self-reflection, and the art of keeping small daily promises. I discovered a system that helped me reclaim balance — a method to beat the madness — and it has worked for years.

Now, I share this holistic approach with those ready to live better, healthier, and happier.

If that sounds like you, I invite you to a complimentary first session — a space to explore your path and see if my coaching aligns with your journey.

Professional Achievements

Professionally, I hold an MBA in Marketing and Finance from Symbiosis Institute of Management Studies and have worked with Citibank in a previous chapter of life. My path later led me into education and hospitality — as Ex Executive Board Member of a school, Managing Director of a heritage hotel, and Director in a private hospitality company.

Over the past 2 decades, I've trained and coached employees across organizations, including GENPACT, and have been part of several train-the-trainer initiatives. I'm also a certified storyteller, coach, and Neuro-Linguistic Practitioner (NLP).

Currently, I'm researching Habit Mastery and integrating it into my coaching framework — blending neuroscience, storytelling, and simple daily rituals that create lasting change.

Along the way, I've had the joy of anchoring and emceeing major events, including two with Dr. Shashi Tharoor, and serving as a Regional Trainer for the networking group BNI. I have trained over 3,000 students and teachers across Jaipur's top schools.

At my core, I'm a constant and curious learner. Today, I bring together real life experience and practical coaching tools, professional training and human connections to help others redesign their inner world and rise stronger.

Because I believe one thing deeply:

You are not broken — you're becoming.

My Coaching Philosophy

My take on coaching is that it is a journey for both the coach and the coachee—much like a beautiful drive filled with aha-moments of self-realization. The coachee is gently guided through their own thoughts until they discover an inner, enchanted garden. There, they plant their favorite self-help tools like flowers. And each day, as they return to this sacred space, those flowers bloom into reminders—guiding them toward deeper self-discovery and inner integration.

One on one coaching benefits:

  • Release limiting beliefs and emotional blocks
  • Build resilience and clarity
  • Reconnect with creativity and purpose

Public Speaking and Communication

Public speaking and communication are powerful skills that open doors in every sphere of life. This coaching is designed to help teachers, students, women, and young adults find their authentic voice, express with clarity, and connect with confidence. The journey is not just about speaking well to others alone but to the self as well—it's about learning to dance to the rhythm of inner confidence.

The Story Tree

This storytelling journey is thoughtfully curated and structured in a sequential manner—designed to nurture both learning and life skills. Each story is carefully chosen to spark curiosity, encourage questioning, and foster a love for reading. For children, it also becomes a joyful way to enhance public speaking skills, build confidence, and gently let go of inhibitions, in a sense it is a special space where all my programs get bundled up to create a lasting experience for young children.
