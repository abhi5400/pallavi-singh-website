✨ About Me ✨

Khamagani! From the vibrant Pink City of Jaipur, where heritage and colours breathe life into everyday stories, I'm Pallavi — a banker turned hotelier, and today, a coach by calling.

My journey with coaching didn't begin in a seminar hall. It began with my natural curiosity about people and their stories — the why behind habits, relationships, and human behaviour. Through my years as a banker and later as a hotelier, one thread remained constant: training and coaching, where I discovered the joy of helping people grow.

My Personal Journey

Life in my twenties and thirties brought its own storm: family complexities, career demands, and personal struggles all colliding. Anxiety, sleepless nights, and helplessness became constant companions. I thought it would never end.

But life taught me this truth: our thoughts may trap us, but our actions can free us.

Step by step, I rebuilt myself — meditation, fitness, small daily promises, and most importantly, a reconnection with myself.

Professional Achievements

Along the way, I trained professionally in communication, became a certified NLP Practitioner and Habit Mastery expert, anchored two book launches of Shri Shashi Tharoor- a personal milestone, served as a Regional Trainer for BNI, Freelance trainer for Genpact and done free lance work with several institutes /businesses in Jaipur.

At my core, I'm a constant and curious learner. Today, I bring together lived experience and professional training to coach individuals. Not as someone who has "arrived," but as someone walking this path alongside you.

My Coaching Philosophy

My take on coaching is that it is a journey for both the coach and the coachee—much like a beautiful drive filled with aha-moments of self-realization. The coachee is gently guided through their own thoughts until they discover an inner, enchanted garden. There, they plant their favorite self-help tools like flowers. And each day, as they return to this sacred space, those flowers bloom into reminders—guiding them toward deeper self-discovery and inner integration.

One on one coaching benefits:

  • Release limiting beliefs and emotional blocks
  • Build resilience and clarity
  • Reconnect with creativity and purpose

Habit Mastery Program

"We are what we repeatedly do. Excellence, then, is not an act but a habit." – Aristotle

Habits have the power to shape us—or break us. They don't just define our routines; they define us. Habits are the lens through which others see us and, more importantly, the lens through which we see ourselves.

Habits are influenced by genetics, nurture, and environment, the good news is—limiting habits can be reduced possibly removed from our routine and New, empowering habits can be built and seamlessly integrated into daily life.

That's exactly why the Habit Mastery Program is designed. Over a span of 3 to 6 months, with periodic revisits every quarter after, we work together until your chosen habits become a natural, lasting part of you.

Public Speaking and Communication

Public speaking and communication are powerful skills that open doors in every sphere of life. This coaching is designed to help teachers, students, women, and young adults find their authentic voice, express with clarity, and connect with confidence. The journey is not just about speaking well to others alone but to the self as well—it's about learning to dance to the rhythm of inner confidence.

The Story Tree

This storytelling journey is thoughtfully curated and structured in a sequential manner—designed to nurture both learning and life skills. Each story is carefully chosen to spark curiosity, encourage questioning, and foster a love for reading. For children, it also becomes a joyful way to enhance public speaking skills, build confidence, and gently let go of inhibitions, in a sense it is a special space where all my programs get bundled up to create a lasting experience for young children.
